Step 1:

Enter your tournament name and the number of teams at your tournament in the form fields indicated by the arrows. Afterwards, hit the submit button. You will not be able to access the next step until all form fields are filled out. If you have an odd number of teams, TrialTrackr will generate and pair a Bye Team automatically.

Step 2:

Enter all team names, number and impermissibles in the form fields indicated by the arrows. Afterwards, hit the submit button.

Step 3:

Determine the number of ballots won that round using the drop-down menu. Enter the ballot results. After clicking the button, you will then be taken to a page with the next rounds' pairings. If a pairing is impermissible, hit the swap button that appears from the top of the screen to bottom.

Step 4:

Repeat step 3 for all remaining rounds. After the fourth round, you will be redirected to the final results of the tournament.
